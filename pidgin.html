How to connect with Pidgin

If you have any problems connecting, fell free to email dale@arandomurl.com

1. Download and install pidgin

You can download it for most platforms at http://pidgin.im. If you are running some linux distributions, it might be installed already (in ubuntu its under Applications > Internet).

2. Register a new account

When opening pidgin it will prompt you to create a new account, or you can go to Tools > Account Manager > Manage Accounts > Add. you should see a dialog that looks like:

Fill in the details as above, changing yourname to the username you would like, and entering your own password. Remember to pick XMPP from the list of protocols and click the "Register new account" checkbox.

Once you have clicked ok, after a moment a dialog should pop up Register New XMPP Account, you can click Register straight away, Thats you registered!

3. Enter the chat room

Now you are registered, make sure our account is online (click the checkbox on the left of your account name in the account manager), then proceed to Buddies > Add Chat from the main pidgin window, you should see a dialog like so:

Make sure to pick the account you registered from the list, fill in the details as shown, your handle is whats displayed in the room. You can check Auto join if you want pidgin to enter the room whenever you are online. Now press add, and double click the web item that just appeared on your buddy list. and you should be here!
